About us

Redgini is Nasik’s first online cake and pastry shop. We are here to serve sweet tooth hunger of Nasik keeping the delicious sweet just a click away. We provide people here a provision to order cakes and pastries online and facilitate home delivery for the same. Our menu includes mouth-watering varieties for it. We follow the principle - ‘Give The Best’ and strive hard to work for the same keeping a guaranteed taste.

Redgini is not only about making and delivering the cakes, we are about creating a wonderful place to work with. We encourage all the "Youngster of Nashik" to join us with this moment and we would love to work with them.
